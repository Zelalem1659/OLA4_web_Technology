Reflection
1. What changed between your Stage 1 outline and your final implementation? Why did you change it?
I kept the same main structure from the outline, but I added a spotlight card on the Home page and a notes panel beside the Blog and New Post pages. Those changes made the layout feel more complete and helped me present the required actions and post details more clearly.

2. What was the hardest part to implement correctly, and why?
The hardest part was keeping the blog data in one place while still using multiple pages. I had to make sure the posts state stayed in App.jsx and was passed down through props so adding and deleting posts updated the correct page immediately.

3. Describe one mistake you made and how you fixed it.
One mistake I made was not trimming the form input values before validation, which could allow users to submit spaces instead of real text. I fixed it by using trim() on the title, author, and content fields before checking whether the form was valid.
